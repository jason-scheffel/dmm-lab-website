Elham's research is on machine learning for nanomaterial design. Specifically her work focuses on generative models trained by experimental data and employed to optimize multiple material properties. She obtained her M.Sc. of Information Technology from the Islamic Azad University, Safashar Branch, Iran in 2017 and BSc of Information Technology from Payame Noor University, Safashahr Branch, Iran in 2013.
