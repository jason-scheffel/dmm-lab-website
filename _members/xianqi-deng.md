Xianqi's research is on Large Language Models for scientific discovery. Specifically, his work focuses on masked language modeling for molecular representation learning, domain-knowledge-informed parameter-efficient fine-tuning, learnable masking for scientific language models, and generative models for nanomaterial design and discovery. He obtained his B.E. in Computer Science and Engineering from North Minzu University, China in 2024.
